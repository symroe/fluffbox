Show instructions

Fluffbox is a tool to help journalists gather links and media assets in the thick of a developing story, later pulling those assets into a new article.

  • Email links & media to fluff@fluffbox.net - emails with the same subject are grouped together - or fave tweets, Flickr photos and Delicious bookmarks.
  • In the editor area, write plain text, HTML or Markdown and drag in your assets.
  • Copy and paste the finished HTML into your CMS.

NOTE: so far only tested in the Chrome browser.
